Lasagna

Description

is a classic Italian dish featuring wide, flat pasta sheets layered with rich fillings like meat (ragù), tomato sauce, creamy béchamel, and cheeses (mozzarella, ricotta, Parmesan), baked until bubbly and golden, forming a hearty casserole. The name refers to both the pasta shape (broad ribbons) and the baked casserole, with countless regional variations from Italy and beyond.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Canned tomatoes
  • Fresh herbs
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Noodles
  • Cheese
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Cook the meat
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the cheese layer
  4. Assemble the lasagna
  5. Bake the lasagna
